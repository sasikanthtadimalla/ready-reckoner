About Ready Reckoner

This ready reckoner is for INSTANT calculation of revenue gain/loss for non-coking coal in grades ranging from G6 to G13 in WCL as per the analyzed grade(s) vis-Ã -vis the ruling prices for ROM grade coal in terms of Price Notification dt. 01.12.2020.

How To Use

Step 1 Select the 'Price Component' - Either ROM (Power) or ROM (Non-Power).
Step 2 Select the 'Billed Grade' from options G6 to G13.
Step 3 Enter the 'Billed Quantity' in 'Lakh Tons'.
Step 4 Enter the percentage of 'Grade Materialization / Slippage' corresponding to the 'Analyzed Grade'.
Step 5 Press 'Calculate' button to view the prices and values. Press 'Reset' to reset the fields.
